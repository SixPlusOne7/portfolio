About Me

I'm a passionate mechanical engineer with expertise in robotics and product design, blending technical precision with creative problem-solving. My journey in engineering began with a fascination for how things work and has evolved into creating innovative solutions that bridge technology and human needs.

With experience in soft robotics, UAV mechanisms, and bio-inspired design, I approach each project with a unique perspective that combines analytical thinking with artistic sensibility. My work at the Soft Robotics and Intelligent Machines Laboratory (SAIL) has honed my skills in both mechanical design and computational analysis.

When I'm not engineering, you can find me exploring digital art, photography, or hiking in nature - activities that continually inspire my technical work.
